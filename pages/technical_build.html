Timber Frame Technical Details

Timber Frame Wall Systems

Our Engineered Timber Wall Systems deliver the highest levels of structural performance, thermal insulation, and airtightness as part of a complete package. With options from an open timber frame panel and factory insulated variants to closed structural insulated panels, we can offer the most cost-effective means of achieving your desired level of performance.

ULTIMA Wall

The continuous Kooltherm insulation lining in our Closed Panel Wall system delivers high levels of thermal performance, airtightness and eliminates cold bridging
  • U Value range 0.18 - 0.09 W/m2K
  • Can exceed building regulation U Values by up to 200%
  • Factory pre-fitted Insulation with Airtight Membrane and Service Void
  • Reduces Cold Bridging, Air Leakage and Drafts
