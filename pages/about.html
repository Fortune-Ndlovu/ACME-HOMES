What is energy efficiency?


Energy efficiency is simply the act of using less energy to perform the same task within a domestic household context, this means a concerted effort to reduce energy consumption, with energy waste of any kind eliminated from our homes. Acme Homes build homes with a bit of forethought and planning and are energy efficient to the benefit of your back pocket and the environment.

Our energy-efficient homes are comfortable and healthy at all times.We need to produce homes that are perfect for our client's needs. Typically warm in winter, cool during the summer times, and also free of drafts. The homes are well ventilated and have excellent air quality. We make sure that the home is never too humid or dry at any given time. Energy efficient, our homeowners ordinarily utilize a programmable thermostat to regulate their heating and cooling operations during the night and at times when they are away from home. Us Acme Homes can help you to upgrade your home to an energy-efficient one and start reaping the benefits today.

In addition to helping the environment by reducing greenhouse emissions. Acme Homes offer solar panels allowing you to keep more money in your bank account each month. You save by using less electricity, a saving that kicks into high gear once you have cut enough in electricity bills to recoup the installation cost.

Our energy-efficient homes have a high energy rating. This means that the homes we build are properly insulated as well as airtight. They have high-efficiency heating and cooling equipment that help to keep the utility bills low. Also outfitted with low-flow accessories to lower water consumption and water heating costs. Those living in this home should keep the inside temperatures reasonable, manage electricity properly as well as buy energy-saving products such as LED bulbs and A rated appliances but we look after our own, we provide the products for you.
